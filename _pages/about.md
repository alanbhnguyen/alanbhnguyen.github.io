Hello! I'm Alan Bao-Huy Nguyen, a PhD. student at Yale University.

My research focuses on the large scale structure of the universe, baryon acoustic oscillations (BAO), galaxy catalogues, and novel statistical methods. I'm involved in both DESI and the Nancy Grace Roman Space Telescope teams.

I was born in the beautiful (and very cold!) city of [Winnipeg](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Winnipeg), Manitoba, and had lived there for the majority of my life. 

Outside of my research life, I enjoy playing basketball and lifting weights. I used to train heavily in martial arts and hold a second degree black belt in Taekwondo from the World Taekwondo Federation. 

I am a classically trained pianist with a significant amount of performing experience, and recently earned an Associate of the Conservatory Canada in Music (ACCM) for piano performance. I am also self taught in a number of other instruments including guitar and cello (and harmonica!)
